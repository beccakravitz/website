about

Becca works throughout the US with Actors' Equity Association theatres and premier talent agencies in their TV/film, on-camera, lifestyle, and voice over divisions. She has been privileged to work on productions and campaigns large and small with local, regional, and national groups. Becca recently portrayed Brigid in Red Herring Productions "The Humans" and was nominated for two Jebby Awards (Best Supporting Actress and Best Production, Play).

Now venturing into the world of video production, Becca is excited to combine her experience on-set and her professional development as a communicator and doer to create the videos she's been privileged to be a part of. She is a flexbile, proactive leader and organizer ready to adapt her expertise to your needs. Information about how to contact Becca for your next video project can be found here.

When not on stage or screen, you can often find Becca traveling with her husband (via Scotts Cheap Flights), gardening with varying amounts of success, or reading a cheesy YA novel.

Becca is a proud Equity Membership Candidate.
